Our Mission

The Mission of STAR Private High School is To foster our students love of learning, sense of wellbeing and acquisition of life skills To empower our students generating practical and theoretical knowledge to better understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities in the 21st Century. To prepare our students to become compassionate global citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

Our Vision To create an environment for students and teachers where we foster a passion for lifelong learning, positive attitude, self-esteem and healthy relationship with family and friends. To become one of the best school in Mandalay, which we prepare our students to attain the best academic achievements, and guide them to choose the right path for their higher education or professional career. To equip our students with the necessary knowledge and skills by embracing innovation and emerging technologies of the 21st century.
